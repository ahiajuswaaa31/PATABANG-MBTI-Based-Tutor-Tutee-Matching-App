About Patabang

Patabang started as a requirement in the Technopreneurship and Idea2Startup Bootcamp in 2023. It won the LGU-Naga Idea2Startup Pitching Challenge and was awarded funding. The project has continued to be developed ever since, and is now implemented in the College of Engineering and Architecture (CEA) of the University of Nueva Caceres (UNC) to help students find suitable tutors.
